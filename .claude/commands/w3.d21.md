## Part 1: Video Prompts

### Questions to Ask (One at a Time)

1. **What you need**: "What video footage do you want to create? B-roll, product shots, atmospheric clips, something else?"

2. **The vision**: "Describe what you want to SEE. What's in frame? What's happening? Don't worry about prompt syntax yet."

3. **The mood**: "What feeling should this evoke? Cinematic, dreamy, energetic, calm, dramatic, luxurious?"

4. **The use**: "Where will this video be used? Brand video, social content, website background, presentation?"

### Video Prompt Structure

```
[Subject] + [Action] + [Camera Movement] + [Style] + [Lighting] + [Duration]
```

Example:
```
"Amber whisky being poured into a crystal glass,
slow motion, close-up tracking shot following the
liquid, cinematic style, warm golden backlighting,
4 seconds"
```

### Camera Movement Options
- **Static**: Fixed camera
- **Pan**: Horizontal movement
- **Tilt**: Vertical movement
- **Tracking**: Following subject
- **Dolly**: Moving towards/away
- **Aerial/drone**: High angle moving
