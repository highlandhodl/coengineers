## Theme 1: Building Your Digital Workspace

**Five labs. One outcome: a system that works.**

By the end of Theme 1, you'll have:

- A conversation pattern with Claude Code that gets results consistently
- An organised folder structure using the PARA method (Projects, Areas, Resources, Archives)
- Reusable document templates you can deploy in seconds
- Metadata systems that make your notes searchable and connected
- A foundation that everything else builds on

**The practical result:** When you sit down to work, you know where everything is. You have templates for common tasks. Your workspace supports what you're trying to do instead of fighting you.

## Theme 2: Content Processing

## What You Won't Get

I want to be honest about limitations:

- You won't become a developer. That's not the goal.
- AI output needs editing. The 80/20 rule applies — AI gets you 80% there, you finish it.
- Some tasks still require human judgment. AI assists, you decide.
- Learning curves exist. Day 1 won't feel like Day 31.

This course teaches practical skills with real limitations. I'd rather you have accurate expectations than inflated ones.
