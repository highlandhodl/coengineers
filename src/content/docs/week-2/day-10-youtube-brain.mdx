## What You'll Learn

- Extract key insights from video transcripts without watching the full video
- Identify actionable takeaways and specific techniques from educational content
- Create study notes and summaries from conference talks or tutorials
- Build custom prompts for different video types

## The Scenario

Jamie watches YouTube videos about whisky marketing, distillery tours, and industry trends. Hours of watching, but nothing captured. Time to extract the value.

## Key Concepts

| Concept                         | Description                                                    |
| ------------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Transcript Access**           | YouTube's built-in transcripts or yt-dlp for downloads         |
| **Content-Specific Extraction** | Different prompts for tutorials, interviews, and presentations |
| **Timestamp Navigation**        | Jump-to guides for key moments worth watching                  |
| **Comparative Analysis**        | Synthesise insights from multiple videos on the same topic     |

## What You'll Create

- Structured study notes from conference talks
- Jump-to guides with key timestamps
- Comparison analyses across multiple videos
- Actionable takeaways from educational content
