By the end of this theme, you'll be creating professional, brand-consistent documents:

- Spreadsheets with formulas and data validation
- Presentations ready to share
- Statements of Work for consulting projects

This theme introduces Claude Code's built-in document skills. The brandkit you create in Lab 1 carries through all subsequent labs, ensuring consistency across everything you produce.

- Completed Themes 1-2 (your workspace and processed content)
