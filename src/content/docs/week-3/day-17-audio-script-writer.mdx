## The Scenario

Thistle & Thorn wants to create audio content: podcast snippets, video voiceovers, and audio guides for distillery tours. ElevenLabs will generate the voice.

## What You'll Create

- Clean scripts for preview (no SSML)
- SSML-formatted scripts for ElevenLabs
- Word count and estimated duration
- Voice recommendations

## Quick Reference

- Write for speaking - read it aloud yourself first
- Include pauses - natural speech has gaps
- Specify target length - know your duration
- Test early - generate a short sample first
