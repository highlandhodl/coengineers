### Pro Tips

**Scope Section:**

- Be very specific about what's included
- Be even MORE specific about what's excluded
- When in doubt, list it as out of scope

**Deliverables:**

- Each deliverable should have clear acceptance criteria
- Client should know exactly what "done" looks like
- If you can't measure it, refine the description

**Payment Terms:**

- Never work for 100% payment at end (too risky)
- Aim for 25-30-30-15 milestone split
- Always get some payment upfront (shows commitment)

**Assumptions:**

- List everything that must be true for your estimate to hold
- Include access, availability, existing conditions
- If an assumption proves false, trigger change process

**Professional Language:**

- Use "Service Provider" and "Client" instead of "we" and "you"
- Stay formal but clear (avoid excessive legalese)
- Define any industry-specific terms

**Legal Protection:**

- Have a solicitor review your template once
- Adjust for your specific industry and risks
- When in doubt, consult legal counsel
