## Common Questions & Answers

### Q: What if I don't have time to practise all these techniques?

**A:** You don't need to use all of them. Pick the 2-3 that solve your biggest pain points and focus on those. The others are there when you need them.

### Q: Should I be processing everything through Claude now?

**A:** No! Use these techniques for content that matters, that you'll reference later, or that's currently creating friction. Simple stuff can stay simple.

### Q: How do I know if I'm "good enough" at these skills?

**A:** If you can process content faster than before and the output is useful to you, you're good enough. Mastery comes with repetition, not perfection on day one.

### Q: Can I go back and redo lessons if I need a refresher?

**A:** Absolutely! Just type the command for that day (e.g., `/w2.d8` for Meeting Tamer) and go through it again. The lessons are always available.
